About Us

WE RESPECT YOU!

Resc-You is a safe and supportive online community for recovering addicts to connect and support each other in their journey towards sobriety.
We are a community of people who have been where you are now, and we are here to help you get where you want to be.
Our sole aim is to provide a comprehensive and holistic approach to recovery, offering a range of tools, resources, and support to help individuals overcome their addiction and achieve long-term sobriety.
The chats are secured using hedera hashgraph, which is a decentralized network which ensures messages are stored in a tamper-proof manner.

Our Mission

"At Resc-You, our mission is to provide a safe and supportive space for recovering addicts to connect, share their experiences, and offer support and encouragement to each other. We believe that recovery is a journey, and we are committed to helping our users every step of the way. Whether you are just starting out on your recovery journey or you are further along the path, Resc-You is here to help you find the support and guidance you need to achieve and maintain sobriety. Together, we can create a strong and caring community that helps everyone on their journey to a sober and fulfilling life."
