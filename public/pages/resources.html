Local Support Groups

Alcoholics Anonymous (AA): AA is a 12-step program that provides support and resources for people dealing with alcohol addiction. The organization has chapters in many cities throughout India, and meetings are typically held on a regular basis.
Narcotics Anonymous (NA): NA is a 12-step program similar to AA, but focused on helping people who are dealing with addiction to drugs. Like AA, NA has chapters in many cities throughout India, and meetings are typically held on a regular basis.
Al-Anon/Alateen: Al-Anon and Alateen are organizations that provide support for family members and loved ones of people dealing with addiction. The organizations have chapters in many cities throughout India, and meetings are typically held on a regular basis.
SMART Recovery: SMART Recovery is a non-12-step program that provides support and resources for people dealing with addiction. The organization has chapters in many cities throughout India, and meetings are typically held on a regular basis.
Celebrate Recovery: Celebrate Recovery is a Christian-based recovery program that uses the 12 steps as a framework. The program has locations in many cities throughout India, and meetings are typically held on a regular basis.

Other Online Communities

Reddit is a popular online platform that allows users to create and participate in online communities, called "subreddits," focused on specific topics or interests. There are many different subreddits dedicated to supporting people dealing with addiction or recovery.
r/StopDrinking: This subreddit is focused on supporting people who are trying to stop drinking alcohol. It provides a space for people to share their experiences, ask for advice, and offer support to others.
r/Recovery: This subreddit is focused on supporting people who are dealing with addiction, recovery, or mental health issues. It provides a space for people to share their experiences and offer support and advice to others.
r/addiction: This subreddit is focused on discussing addiction and recovery, and provides a space for people to share their experiences and ask for advice.
r/Alcoholics: This subreddit is focused on supporting people who are dealing with alcohol addiction. It provides a space for people to share their experiences and offer support and advice to others.
